# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 001
**Title:** Faulty Electrical: A Shocking End in Bangkok
**Slug:** faulty-electrical
**Category:** Environmental Hazards

## The Death (One Sentence)
A tech entrepreneur charging his laptop in a rain-damaged hostel bathroom is electrocuted when faulty wiring turns a puddle into a death trap.

## Tagline
"In the startup world, they called him a disruptor. The 220 volts disrupted him right back."

## Themes
1. Tech hubris vs. infrastructure reality - the belief that innovation conquers all, even basic physics
2. The dangers of "roughing it" when you've never actually roughed it
3. First World solutions in Third World wiring

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 35%
- Horror: 40%
- Satire: 25%

## Target Reader
Travelers who've ever charged their phone in a sketchy outlet, anyone who's noticed flickering lights in a cheap hostel, and tech workers who think their Macbook is invincible.

## Comparison Titles
- *The Social Network* meets *Final Destination*
- Tucker Max's travel stories meets *Saw*

## Content Warnings
- Graphic electrocution
- Body horror (electrical burns)
- Brief depictions of cardiac arrest
